Alerts

Those come useful when you need to display some short, but important alerts or notifications. They need to be simple and visible.

There are 4 types of alert boxes: .alert, .alert.success, .alert.error, .alert.warning.
You can see what they look like below, in this order.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet
<div class="alert">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet

This alert can be dismissed.
Adding the .dismiss class enables a close button to the left of the alert.

<div class="alert dismiss">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet

The animation is here looped and made slower for the purpose of the demo.
They also come with a nice appearing animation if you add the .fadin class.

Tables

Three different sizes

To be used with the following classes : table.small, table.medium, and table.big. Medium size is used by default.

<table class="small">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25
<table class="medium">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25
<table class="big">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25

Alternating rows and columns

Alternating background colors for rows and lines can be set using the following classes :

  • table.alt-row for rows,
  • table.alt-col for columns,
  • You can also combine both.

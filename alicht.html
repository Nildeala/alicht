About the Project

It's a community-driven, free price web hosting platform for the cool kids.

Our servers are powered by free, open-source software and financed by donations only, so you won't find any ads here.

We aim to provide secure and transparent communication tools (e.g., email, web space, XMPP) for our various users who will be part of a community of open web enthusiasts and other awesome fellows.

Tables

Three different sizes

To be used with the following classes : table.small, table.medium, and table.big. Medium size is used by default.

<table class="small">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25
<table class="medium">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25
<table class="big">
1 2 3 4 5
2 4 6 8 10
3 6 9 12 15
4 8 12 16 20
5 10 15 20 25

Alternating rows and columns

Alternating background colors for rows and lines can be set using the following classes :

  • table.alt-row for rows,
  • table.alt-col for columns,
  • You can also combine both.

<table>
Username Full name Action
fabienwang Fabien Wang
younishd Younis Bensalah
Schoewilliam William Dorffer

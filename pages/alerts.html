Alerts

Those come useful when you need to display some short, but important alerts or notifications. They need to be simple and visible.

There are 4 types of alert boxes: .alert, .alert.success, .alert.error, .alert.warning.
You can see what they look like below, in this order.

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet
<div class="alert">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet

This alert can be dismissed.
Adding the .dismiss class enables a close button to the left of the alert.

<div class="alert dismiss">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</div>
